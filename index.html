The Basics

Cholesterol Decoded

✅

HDL — Good

Sweeps cholesterol away from arteries. Higher is better.

Target: 40+ mg/dL
⚠️

LDL — Bad

Builds dangerous plaque in artery walls. Lower is better.

Target: below 100
🔥

Triglycerides

Blood fat from excess sugar & refined carbs.

Target: below 150
🧬

Root Causes

What's Driving Numbers Up?

🍚

Refined Carbs

White rice 2–3x daily. Liver converts excess into triglycerides.

🧈

Trans Fats

Samosas, vanaspati, reused oil — plaque's best friend.

🍬

Hidden Sugar

Sweet chai ×4 cups + mithai = triglyceride bomb.

🪑

Sitting All Day

Only 10% urban Indians meet exercise guidelines.

😰

Chronic Stress

Cortisol spikes LDL production & visceral fat.

🧬

Genetics

South Asian predisposition to insulin resistance.

💡

Good News

70–80% of risk is modifiable. Lifestyle alone can match medication effectiveness.

Indian Diet Guide

Eat Smart, Eat Indian

No exotic superfoods needed. Your kitchen already has the answers.

🫘

Oats & Barley

Beta-glucan traps cholesterol. Oats chilla or daliya for breakfast.

↓ LDL 5–10%
🌿

Methi Seeds

Soak 1 tsp overnight, drink water in morning. Clinically proven.

↓ LDL 10–15%
🌾

Millets (Ragi, Jowar)

8× more fibre than white rice. Replace your rotis.

↓ Triglycerides
🐟

Fish (Bangda, Rawas)

Omega-3 powerhouse. Eat 2–3 times per week.

↓ TG 20–30%
🥜

Almonds & Walnuts

30g daily. Walnuts are richest in plant omega-3.

↓ LDL 3–5%
🧄

Garlic (Lehsun)

2–3 raw cloves daily. Inhibits cholesterol synthesis.

↓ Total 10–15%
🫚

Haldi + Black Pepper

Curcumin kills oxidised LDL. Pepper boosts absorption 2000%.

Anti-inflammatory
🥄

Flaxseed (Alsi)

1 tbsp ground daily in roti dough or curd.

↓ LDL 8–14%
🍋

Amla

Richest Vitamin C source. 1–2 daily or as juice.

↓ Cholesterol
